Applied Analysis Day 2025

University of Ottawa, October 10, 2025

The Applied Analysis Day is an annual event co-organised by the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Ottawa and the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Carleton University since 2017, with the organizing committee alternating between the two departments. This year, it will be held at the University of Ottawa.

The main goal of this one-day event is to bring together Ottawa-area applied math researchers, including graduate students, to exchange ideas and build community.

General information

Date: October 10, 2025
Time: from 8:30am to 17:30pm
Location: STM 464, 150 Louis-Pasteur Private, Ottawa, ON K1N 9A7
Keynote speakers: Lia Bronsard and Jean-Phillipe Lessard
Organizers: Diane Guignard, Tanya Schmah, and Cristina Stoica
